Banned Books

What Are Banned Books?

Banned books are books that have been removed or restricted in certain areas due to their content.

How You Can Help

Learn more about the importance of free expression and take action to prevent book banning.

Examples of Banned Books

  • 1984 by George Orwell
  • The Catcher in theRye by J.D. Salinger
  • Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling

Why I Created This Page

I created this page to raise awareness about banned books and the importance of keeping literature accessible to everyone.
