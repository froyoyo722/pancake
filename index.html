What Are Book Bans?

Book bans are attempts to remove or restrict access to books. Many of these books address important topics like race, identity, or historical events.

Why I Made This Page

I created this site to spread awareness about book bans and how they affect students, communities, and education. Everyone deserves the freedom to read and learn.

How You Can Help

  • Talk about book bans with friends and family.
  • Attend school board meetings or public forums.
  • Read banned books and share them with others.

Banned Books

  • The Hate U Give by Angie Thomas – Banned for its themes of police violence.
  • Maus by Art Spiegelman – Banned for "inappropriate language" despite its historical importance.
  • Gender Queer by Maia Kobabe – Banned due to LGBTQ+ content.
